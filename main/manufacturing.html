Our Facilities

Three World-Class Factories

Strategically located for maximum reach, built for maximum quality.

We operate three advanced manufacturing units, each equipped with high-efficiency production lines managed by skilled professionals. Our facilities handle large-scale production of plastic pipes while maintaining environmental responsibility and strict safety standards. This capacity allows us to innovate rapidly, scale to demand, and maintain consistent quality across every batch shipped across India and beyond.

Step by Step

The Manufacturing Process

Step 01 — Raw Material Selection

Material Sourcing & Testing

Raw materials — high-grade polymers, UV stabilizers, and coloring agents — are sourced from verified suppliers and subjected to rigorous quality testing before any production begins. Only approved materials enter our production line.

Step 02 — Extrusion & Molding

High-Tech Extrusion

Materials are melted at precise temperatures and extruded through custom molds to form pipe structures of various diameters and wall thicknesses. Our PLC-controlled extruders ensure dimensional accuracy at every meter produced.

Step 03 — Cooling & Sizing

Precision Cooling & Haul-Off

Freshly extruded pipes pass through calibrated cooling baths and haul-off machines that maintain exact outer dimensions and wall uniformity. This step locks in the pipe's shape, flexibility, and pressure rating characteristics.

Step 04 — Quality Testing & Packaging

Testing, Cutting & Dispatch

Final pipes undergo hydrostatic pressure tests, dimensional checks, and surface inspections. Approved products are cut to length, coiled or bundled, labeled with batch traceability codes, and dispatched to distributors across India.

Technology

Advanced Machinery & Automation

Our production floor features a full array of industrial equipment: twin-screw and single-screw extruders, vacuum cooling tanks, servo-driven haul-off units, automatic pipe cutters, and precision coil winders. Every machine is controlled via PLC systems that monitor output in real time — minimizing waste and ensuring batch-to-batch consistency.

This level of automation not only boosts throughput but enables rapid changeovers between pipe sizes and materials — giving our clients the flexibility of custom manufacturing at industrial scale.

Raw Materials

What Goes Into Every Pipe

We use polymers derived from certified PE 80, PE 100, and reinforced PVC compounds sourced exclusively from verified suppliers. Our materials undergo melt flow index testing and tensile strength verification before entering production.

The combination of road-stone-grade strength polymers and flexible compounds results in pipes that withstand pressure fluctuations, temperature extremes, UV exposure, and rough handling — making them equally suitable for domestic gardens and heavy industrial applications.
