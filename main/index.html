Your Reliable Partner in Plastic Pipe Solutions

Ranjana Plastic is a leading manufacturer and wholesaler of premium plastic pipes, based in the Mandore Industrial Area, Jodhpur. We serve agriculture, construction, and infrastructure sectors with pipes that combine innovation, durability, and cost-efficiency.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility uses advanced extrusion technology and PLC-controlled systems to produce HDPE, LDPE, PVC, CPVC, MDPE, and braided pipes — all meeting ISO, BIS, and ROHS standards.
